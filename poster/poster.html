APOLLO on Small GPT-2: Can Structured Scaling Beat the AdamW vs GaLore Tradeoff?

We reproduce the core APOLLO story on a GPT-2 124M setup: AdamW is accurate but memory-hungry, GaLore is memory-efficient but SVD-heavy, and SGD is cheap but weak for Transformers. APOLLO claims to keep AdamW-level quality while moving toward SGD-like memory cost.

The Dilemma

  • AdamW: good convergence, poor memory footprint.
  • GaLore: lower optimizer memory, but SVD adds compute spikes.
  • SGD + Momentum: fast and light, yet unstable for Transformer pretraining.
  • APOLLO: structured scaling + random projection.
